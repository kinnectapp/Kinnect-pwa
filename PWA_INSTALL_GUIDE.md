# 📱 Kinnect PWA Installation Guide

Your Kinnect app is a Progressive Web App (PWA) - it works like a native mobile app but runs in your browser!

## 🚀 How to Install on Your Phone

### **Android (Chrome, Edge, Brave)**

1. **Open Kinnect in your browser**
   - Visit the Kinnect website on your Android phone

2. **Install the app**
   - You'll see an **"Install app"** prompt at the bottom
   - OR tap the 3-dot menu (⋯) → **"Install app"**

3. **Done!**
   - The app appears on your home screen
   - Tap it to open - it works like a native app!

### **iOS (Safari)**

1. **Open Kinnect in Safari**
   - Visit Kinnect on your iPhone/iPad using Safari

2. **Add to Home Screen**
   - Tap the **Share button** (⬆️ from bottom)
   - Scroll and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
   - Name it "Kinnect"
   - Tap **"Add"**

3. **Done!**
   - The app appears on your home screen
   - Tap it to launch!

## ✨ Features of Installed PWA

After installing, you get:

- ✅ **App Icon** - Custom icon on home screen
- ✅ **Standalone Mode** - No address bar, looks like native app
- ✅ **Offline Support** - Works offline (messages cached)
- ✅ **Push Notifications** - Get notified of new matches and messages
- ✅ **Fast Loading** - Previously visited pages load instantly
- ✅ **No App Store** - No download limits, instant updates
- ✅ **Full Screen** - Immersive mobile experience

## 🔄 Updates

When you visit Kinnect, the app automatically checks for updates. There's no "waiting for app store approval" - updates happen instantly!

## 🗑️ Uninstall

**Android:** Long-press the app → "Remove from home screen"  
**iOS:** Long-press the app → "Remove App" → "Remove from Home Screen"

---

## ❓ Troubleshooting

**Not seeing install prompt?**

- Make sure you're on HTTPS (secure connection)
- Try Chrome, Edge, or Brave browser
- Scroll down - the prompt might be at the bottom

**App not updating?**

- Uninstall and reinstall the app
- Or clear your browser cache

**Offline mode not working?**

- Open your browser settings
- Allow offline access for Kinnect
